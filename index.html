About the data

This data was collected and processed by Zillow and provides insight into how affordable major US cities are for their residents. In the display above, a city's population size is shown by its size (larger circle = larger population), and its affordability is shown through circle color. Both the rent affordability and mortgage affordability values shown above incorporate median income data from the given city. Because of this, it is important to consider that the values do not indicate how affordable a city may be to move to, as someone moving from a one city would be less likely to align with the income statistics of a different city.
